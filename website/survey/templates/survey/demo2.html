Everything you want to know about

Survey System

Change your account details

Once you login, you can change your first name, last name and email if you want. Click on the edit profile button and you can see a form to change all the details Once you are done editing, click on submit and your details will be updated successfully.

Forgot Password

Don't remember your password ? Dont worry, enter your verification details correctly and you will recieve a mail which contains the auto generated password. You can change it later according to your convenience.

Create Survey

Now it's time to create your Survey ! Just login to the system and click on "Create Survey", you can add any number of questions as per your need and click on Submit button. All you have to do next is to wait untill your survey gets approved. Then your survey will be published!

Get Results

Excited to know the results of your survey ? Just go to your surveys and click the "get results" button beside the survey name whose results you want to know. You will be taken to a page which will display the total number of votes per a particular option.

Withdraw or Cancel Survey

If you want to withdraw your survey before it gets published or cancel a published survey, you can do that too. Just go to your surveys and you will see withdraw and cancel options beside every survey. Click on one the you want to delete and done! Also, you will get an email notification regarding the action you've done.

Participate

Inorder to participate in any surveys first you have to login to the system. You can see the list of surveys that you can take part in and the participate button for each survey. Just click on the participate button, you can see the survey form which displays all the questions in the survey. Once you choose the options click on Submit and done!
